IAT

The next task is the IAT. In this task, you will see words and images, presented one at a time at the center of the screen. The categories will be presented at the top-right and top-left corners of the screen. For each word or image, if it belongs to a category on the right, hit the key i, and if it belongs to a category on the left, hit the key e.
